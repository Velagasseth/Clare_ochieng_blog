About Clare Ochieng

Clare ochieng is an award-winning investigative journalist based in Nairobi, Kenya. With over 10 years of experience in the field, she has uncovered numerous stories of public interest and brought about meaningful change through her reporting.

Professional Background

Clare began her career as a reporter for The Daily Nation before moving into investigative journalism. She has worked with international media organizations including BBC Africa and Reuters, specializing in political corruption, human rights, and environmental issues.

Awards and Recognition

  • 2022 African Journalist of the Year
  • 2021 Media Council of Kenya Excellence in Investigative Reporting
  • 2019 Thomson Foundation Young Journalist Award
